The TakaScore Engine

We turn informal transaction history into a bankable credit score. Our proprietary algorithm de-risks the "unbankable" waste sector.

Why TakaLink Wins

  • Asset-Light: No heavy capex on trucks or balers. We scale via data.
  • Network Moat: Exclusive MOU with TERECSO locks the supply side data.
  • First Mover: Only platform in EA providing verified EPR compliance reports.

Execution Risks

  • Data Integrity: Risk of fraudulent entries at collection points. (Mitigated by GPS + photo verification).
  • Adoption: Resistance from informal aggregators to digital transparency.
  • Regulatory: Changes in EPR laws or plastic ban enforcement.
